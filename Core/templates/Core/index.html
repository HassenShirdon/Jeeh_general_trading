About

Find Out More About Us

Jeeh General Trading is a full-service logistics and transportation import and export company based in Djibouti, UAE, and Turkey.

Why Choose us?

We plan to differentiate ourselves by providing customized logistics solutions tailored to our clients' specific needs, offering competitive pricing, and focusing on customer satisfaction. We will also leverage technology to improve our operations and provide real-time visibility into our clients' shipments.

  • Mission

    Our target market includes importers, exporters, manufacturers, distributors, and retailers across various industries, such as retail, automotive, foods and consumer goods. The logistics and transportation industry is highly competitive, and our main competitors include established global players and local providers.

  • Vision

    Our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective logistics solutions to our clients across the globe. Our services include air, sea, and land transportation, customs clearance, warehousing, packaging, and distribution
